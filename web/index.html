Ficus was an app for making online presentations with real-time polls and instant feedback from the audience. It was built by a group of friends in 2014.

Though the app is no longer available, we hope to keep the memory of our creation alive and inspire others to pursue their dreams of building something fun.

During the demolition, we extracted a standalone ESM module that can be used to embed these polls as widgets on any website.

Enjoy hacking!

Ficus was planted, nurtured, and eventually withered away by Code Hipsters: Eugene, Sam, Tikhon, Alexey, Victor and Boris.
